## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Is my data safe?
**A**: Yes. Data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database. Cloud backups are redundant and encrypted. You own your data.

### Q: Can I use offline?
**A**: Yes. Bright Ops is offline-first. Work without internet for up to 7 days. Sync automatically when back online. All tiers have the same 7-day grace period.

### Q: Can multiple users use the same account?
**A**: Yes, but each user needs their own account. Starter allows 1 user, Pro allows 5 users, Enterprise unlimited. All sync in real-time.

### Q: What if I need more devices?
**A**: Starter allows 1 device binding. Pro allows 2. Enterprise allows unlimited. You can contact support to rebind devices.

### Q: Do you have mobile app?
**A**: Currently Windows desktop only. iOS/Android apps planned for Q3 2026.

### Q: What about my current data?
**A**: Import CSV spreadsheets easily. We can also help migrate data from other systems (Pro/Enterprise).

### Q: Is there a free version?
**A**: 30-day free trial with all features. Starter plan has perpetual free tier access after trial ends (some limits).

### Q: What about support?
**A**: Starter gets email support. Pro gets priority email support. Enterprise gets 24/7 phone, chat, and email.

### Q: Can I export my data?
**A**: Yes. All plans allow CSV export. Enterprise also allows API access for custom exports.

### Q: What if billing fails?
**A**: We'll send email reminders. You get a 15-day grace period before access is restricted. Update payment method anytime.

### Q: Do you offer refunds?
**A**: Yes. 30-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked.
